Chocolate Cake

Description

A decadent and moist chocolate cake that's perfect for any celebration. Rich, fluffy, and covered in smooth chocolate frosting.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Baking powder
  • Vanilla extract
  • Salt
  • Chocolate frosting

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add the eggs, milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth.
  4. Divide the batter evenly between the prepared pans and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  5. Let the cakes cool completely before frosting with chocolate frosting.
  6. Slice and serve with a dollop of whipped cream or a scoop of ice cream.
