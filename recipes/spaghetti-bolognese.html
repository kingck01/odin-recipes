Spaghetti Bolognese

Description

A rich and savory Italian pasta dish featuring a slow-cooked meat sauce with tomatoes, onions, and garlic. A comforting favorite for all ages.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Olive oil
  • Red wine
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, carrots, and celery. Cook until softened.
  2. Add the ground beef and cook until browned.
  3. Pour in the red wine and simmer until reduced by half.
  4. Add the tomato sauce, tomato paste, and Italian seasoning. Simmer on low heat for at least an hour.
  5. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  6. Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti. Top with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
