Caesar Salad

Description

A classic salad with crisp romaine lettuce, creamy Caesar dressing, crunchy croutons, and grated Parmesan cheese. Perfect as a side or main dish.

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Caesar dressing
  • Chicken breast (optional)
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Anchovy paste (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the romaine lettuce. Set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the Caesar dressing, lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, and anchovy paste (if using).
  3. Add the lettuce to the bowl and toss to coat with the dressing.
  4. Top with croutons and grated Parmesan cheese. Add grilled chicken slices if desired.
  5. Serve immediately. Enjoy your fresh and crunchy Caesar Salad!
