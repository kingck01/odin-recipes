Lasagna

Description

A classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and a creamy cheese filling. Perfect for a hearty family dinner.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, cook the ground beef until browned.
  4. Add the tomato sauce, garlic, and onion to the skillet and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Layer the noodles, meat sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  6. Bake for 25 minutes or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
